Phishing Awareness Alert

⚠️ This was a security test ⚠️

You just interacted with a simulated phishing exercise

What you should know:

  • Never enter credentials on suspicious login pages
  • Check URL authenticity - look for misspellings or wrong domains
  • Hover over links to preview destination before clicking
  • Verify unexpected requests through another channel (e.g. phone call)
  • Report suspicious emails using the "Report Phishing" button in Outlook

Why this matters:

  • 91% of cyber attacks start with phishing emails
  • Successful phishing costs companies $4.65 million on average
  • Training reduces phishing susceptibility by 60%
