About phishing

This page explains common phishing tactics and how to protect your account.

Red flags to watch for

  • Suspicious domains or look‑alike URLs (extra letters, unusual TLDs).
  • Urgent language pushing you to act immediately.
  • Requests for credentials or payment out of context.

How to protect yourself

  • Check the address bar and only sign in on trusted domains.
  • Enable multi‑factor authentication and use strong, unique passwords.
  • Report suspicious messages and avoid downloading unknown attachments.

If you think you were phished

  • Change your password immediately and revoke any suspicious sessions.
  • Review connected apps and API tokens; remove anything you don't recognize.
  • Contact support with details (URLs, emails, screenshots) for investigation.
